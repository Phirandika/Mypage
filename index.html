Public Health Information

  • Dengue Prevention: Remove stagnant water weekly. Clean gutters and cover containers.
  • Food Safety: Ensure clean cooking environments, safe water, and proper food storage.
  • Personal Hygiene: Wash hands regularly with soap, especially before eating and after using the toilet.
  • Waste Management: Separate organic and plastic waste. Compost biodegradable waste.
  • Vector Control: Participate in community cleanup programs and report mosquito breeding grounds.
